免責聲明 / Disclaimer
本指南是根據 POEM 分數、症狀頻率閾值及環境修飾因子所建立的層級決策樹而制定，用於日常濕疹護理參考。請務必優先聽取醫生的專業建議。遵循這些指南並不能保證在所有情況下都能成功治療。此外，這些指南不應被解釋為設定了治療標準，也不應被視為涵蓋所有適當的治療方法，或排除其他旨在獲得相同結果的合理治療方法。任何特定療法的適用性最終判斷必須由醫生和患者根據患者的具體情況以及疾病已知的變異性和生物學行為而做出。本指南反映了指南制定時可獲得的最佳數據。未來的研究結果可能需要對本指南中的建議進行修訂，以反映新的數據。 This guideline is derived from a hierarchical decision tree based on POEM scores, symptom frequency thresholds, and environmental modifiers, intended for daily eczema care. Please always prioritize medical advice from your doctor. Adherence to these guidelines will not ensure successful treatment in every situation. In addition, these guidelines should not be interpreted as setting a standard of care, or be deemed inclusive of all proper methods of care nor exclusive of other methods of care reasonably directed to obtaining the same results. The ultimate judgment regarding the propriety of any specific therapy must be made by the physician and the patient in light of all the circumstances presented by the individual patient and the known variability and biologic behavior of the disease. This guideline reflects the best available data at the time the guideline was prepared. The results of future studies may require revisions to the recommendations in this guideline to reflect new data.
